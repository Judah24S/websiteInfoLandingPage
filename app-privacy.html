App Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Boom-In mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Your name and email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Profile information and bio
  • Educational background and professional history
  • Photos and user-generated content
  • Messages and communications within the app

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, OS version, device ID)
  • Usage data (features accessed, time spent, interactions)
  • Location data (if you grant permission)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address and network information
  • Push notification engagement data

Permissions We Request

  • Camera - for profile photos and video features
  • Photo Library - for uploading images
  • Contacts - to help you find and connect with peers
  • Location - to provide location-based networking features
  • Push Notifications - for important updates and messages
  • Calendar - for event integration (if applicable)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide networking and connection features
  • Send push notifications and messages
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Conduct analytics and understand user behavior
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help operate the app (analytics, hosting, payment processors)
  • Other users you choose to connect with on the platform
  • Law enforcement if required by law

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party services such as analytics providers and social media platforms. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in Europe, you have rights including access, correction, erasure, and data portability. We will not process your data on legal basis of consent without explicit consent.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or update your information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Disable location tracking in app settings
  • Revoke app permissions through your device settings

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at boominnetworkenterprise@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if the changes are significant, by sending you a notification within the app.
