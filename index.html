About Boom-In

Founded by former alumni who experienced firsthand how university networks fade after graduation. Boom-In transforms that problem into opportunity by creating vibrant digital communities where students connect with alumni and meaningful professional relationships continue to flourish.

We're building the go-to platform where students find mentorship, alumni discover career opportunities, and everyone unlocks the full potential of their university network. Our vision is simple: connect every student with the alumni network they need, and help alumni stay engaged with their alma mater.
